Beaches
There are two public beaches in Taniti the   Yellow Leaf Bay  and  Merriton Landing Beach.  Both beaches are free to visit and open 24 hours however it is discouraged to be in the water after dark. We strongly encourage to check beach condtions before your visit so your can plan accordingly. Please be aware of wildlife and refrain from feeding them.

Rainforest
Our rainforest located near the center of the island is home to many native species. Please use caution when exploring the rainforest and stay on the marked trails. Please check weather conditions before heading out to ensure you have the proper clothing. There are three outpost stationed on the edges of the forest. Please check in with the forest rangers prior to entry so they can know how many people are in your party.

Around the city
Come visit Merriton Landing!
This rapidly growing part of Taniti is home to several pubs including a microbrewery. The Dancing Parrot is a dance club in the heart of Merriton Landings night life. Family friendly palces include  The Volcanic Arcade  an arcade with games for all ages. Also the is the  Star Theater, the  Coconut Bowling Alley, and the  Taniti Art Gallery.
